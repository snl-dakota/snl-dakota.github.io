truth_surrogate_bypass
""""""""""""""""""""""


Bypass lower level surrogates when performing truth verifications on a top level surrogate


.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1



**Specification**

- *Alias:* None

- *Arguments:* None

- *Default:* no bypass


**Description**


``truth_surrogate_bypass`` (a flag for bypassing all lower level

surrogate).
