gr_threshold
""""""""""""


Convergence tolerance for the Gelman-Rubin statistic



**Topics**


bayesian_calibration


.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1



**Specification**

- *Alias:* None

- *Arguments:* REAL

- *Default:* 1.2


**Description**


The gr_threshold is the convergence tolerance for the Gelman-Rubin statistic which will govern the
convergence of the multiple chain process.
