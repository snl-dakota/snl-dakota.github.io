"""""
naive
"""""


Take the highest scoring candidates


.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1



**Specification**

- *Alias:* None

- *Arguments:* None


**Description**


This strategy will select the ``n`` highest scoring candidates regardless of their
position. This tends to group an entire round of points in the same area.
