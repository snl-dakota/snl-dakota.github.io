""""""""""""""""""""""""""
average_estimator_variance
""""""""""""""""""""""""""


Utilize the estimator variance averaged over the QoI as the solver metric for sampling-based multifidelity methods.



.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1



**Specification**

- *Alias:* None

- *Arguments:* None


**Description**


The average estimator variance option treats each QoI more equally, as
compared to other max and norm options for scalar reduction of the
estimator variance vector.
