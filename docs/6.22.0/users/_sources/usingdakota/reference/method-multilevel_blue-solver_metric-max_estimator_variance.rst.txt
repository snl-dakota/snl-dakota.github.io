""""""""""""""""""""""
max_estimator_variance
""""""""""""""""""""""


Utilize the maximum estimator variance as the solver metric for sampling-based multifidelity methods.



.. toctree::
   :hidden:
   :maxdepth: 1



**Specification**

- *Alias:* None

- *Arguments:* None


**Description**


The maximum estimator variance option focuses strictly on the worst case variance, 
relative to the other average and norm options for scalar reduction of the estimator
variance vector.
